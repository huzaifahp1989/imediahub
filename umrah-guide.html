Step-by-step guide for performing Umrah

Introduction to Umrah

Umrah is a pilgrimage to Makkah that can be performed at any time of the year. While not obligatory like Hajj, it is a highly recommended act of worship that brings immense spiritual rewards.

The Prophet ï·º said: "Umrah to Umrah is an expiation for what is between them." (Sahih Bukhari & Muslim)

Step 1: Before Entering Ihram

Preparation:

  • Make sincere intention (Niyyah) for Umrah
  • Perform ghusl (full body purification) if possible
  • Trim nails and remove unwanted hair
  • Apply perfume (before entering Ihram, not after)
  • Men: Wear two white, unstitched cloths (Izar and Rida)
  • Women: Wear modest Islamic clothing (any color)

Step 2: Enter Ihram at the Miqat

The Miqat:

The Miqat is the designated boundary where pilgrims must enter into the state of Ihram before proceeding to Makkah. Each direction has its specific Miqat.

Entering Ihram:

  • Make intention: "Labbayka Allahumma bi 'Umrah" (Here I am O Allah, for Umrah)
  • Pray two rak'ahs (if possible, not obligatory)
  • Begin reciting the Talbiyah

Step 3: Perform Tawaf (Circling the Ka'bah)

How to Perform Tawaf:

  1. Begin at the Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad): Face the Black Stone, raise your hands and say "Bismillahi Allahu Akbar"
  2. Touch or point: If possible, kiss or touch the Black Stone. If not, point towards it with your right hand.
  3. Circle the Ka'bah: Walk counter-clockwise around the Ka'bah, keeping it on your left
  4. Men: Walk briskly (Raml) and uncover the right shoulder (Idtiba) during first three circuits only
  5. Complete 7 circuits: Each time you reach the Black Stone, raise your hands and say "Allahu Akbar"
  6. Rukn al-Yamani: Touch this corner if possible (don't kiss it), or continue without pointing

  • Drink Zamzam water and make dua

Step 4: Perform Sa'i (Walking Between Safa and Marwah)

How to Perform Sa'i:

  1. Start at Safa: Climb Safa hill, face the Ka'bah, and make dua
  2. Walk to Marwah: Walk (men jog between green lights) to Marwah - this is one lap
  3. At Marwah: Climb, face the Ka'bah, and make dua
  4. Return to Safa: Walk back to Safa - this is the second lap
  5. Complete 7 laps: Safa to Marwah = 1, Marwah to Safa = 2, and so on until 7

Step 5: Halq or Qasr (Shaving or Trimming Hair)

Completing Umrah:

  • Men: Shave the entire head (Halq - more rewarding) OR trim at least 1 inch from all over (Qasr)
  • Women: Trim approximately one inch from the ends of their hair (do not shave)

After this, you have completed your Umrah! All restrictions of Ihram are now lifted, and you may return to normal clothing and activities.

Final Notes

  • Be patient and kind to fellow pilgrims
  • Keep your focus on worship and avoid arguments
  • Pray in the Haram as much as possible (immense rewards)
  • Visit Masjid Nabawi in Madinah if able
  • Make sincere dua for yourself, family, and all Muslims
  • Return home with a commitment to maintain good deeds

May Allah accept your Umrah and grant you the best of this world and the Hereafter. Ameen.
