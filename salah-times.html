About Prayer Times

Prayer times are calculated using the Aladhan API, which provides accurate Islamic prayer times based on your location and preferred calculation method.

Calculation Methods

Different Islamic organizations use slightly different calculations for Fajr and Isha times based on the angle of the sun. Choose the method commonly used in your region or recommended by your local mosque.

Asr Calculation

Standard: Used by Shafi'i, Maliki, and Hanbali schools - Asr begins when an object's shadow equals its length plus its shadow at noon.

Hanafi: Asr begins when an object's shadow is twice its length plus its shadow at noon.
